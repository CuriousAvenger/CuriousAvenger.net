About Me

Hello there!! My name is Sai Praneth aka Curious Avenger, and I'm a junior at Bothell High School from the state of Washington. I'm a passionate 16 year old with interests ranging from computer science - specifically in AI, machine learning, bot development, and cybersecurity - to astrophysics - dealing with the study of black holes, physical cosmology, and quantum physics - and a bit of tennis when time persists.

I am self-taught in terms of my computer science or astrophysics knowledge because physics & computers are something that still fascinates me on how mysterious our universe can be and how creative our minds can get when coding. I also volunteer in a non-profit organization called STEM league, which helps kids from ages 10-16 learn to code. I am especially passionate about astrophysics, which is why I will be pursuing a degree (mostly a Ph.D.) in astrophysics & space sciences in the coming years.
