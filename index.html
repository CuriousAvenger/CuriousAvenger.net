What I do

From a young age, I have been fascinated by the intricacies of space and time, the enigmatic nature of black holes, and the boundless expanse of the universe. As I have delved deeper into the field of astrophysics, I have come to realize that there is an abundance of knowledge yet to be uncovered. My passion for this subject has driven me to engage in self-guided research and study, as evidenced by my personal blogs on the topic. I firmly believe that the fields of physics and computer science are intimately interconnected and that pursuing both of them enhances my critical thinking abilities, bringing me closer to unlocking the secrets of the cosmos.
